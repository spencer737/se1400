Audience

Where will the audience be located?

Anywhere that the web is available, but predominately Southern Utah as that is where my art pieces are located.

What age is your target audience?

My target audience is anyone of any age. I want to be able to sell my art to anyone who is interested.

What gender is your target audience?

Both genders are welcome to visit my website.

What racial demographic or ethnicity is your target audience?

I want anyone of any racial background and ethnicity to browse my website and enjoy the content.
